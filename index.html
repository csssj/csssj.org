Welcome, Visitors!

The Cactus and Succulent Society of San Jose (CSSSJ) welcomes you to our website.

The CSSSJ is a diverse group of people with a mutual interest in and fascination for cacti and other succulent plants.

Succulent plants are among the most bizarre, beautiful, and varied in the entire plant kingdom. If you share our interest in these fascinating and unique plants, or just want to learn more, we invite you to join us at our meetings and have some fun.

If you’d like to become a member, you can do so for $15 a person, or $20 a couple. See the Membership Page for additional details, as well as the membership form.

Upcoming Events

Club Meeting: Sunday, April 3rd, 2022, 12:30–2:30 PM at Emma Prusch Farm Park, Meeting Hall, 647 S King Rd, San Jose.

CSSSJ Inaugural Plant Swap!

The Meeting Hall will be open around 12pm. Space is limited. If capacity has been reached, you may be asked to wait to enter.

COVID Precautions: for the safety of all in attendance, please wear a mask when indoors.

Agenda:

12-12:30:Let’s get reacquainted!
12:30:General meeting begins
1:00:Plant Swap commences
2:30:Raffle and Cleanup

Note: Plants may be brought in until 1:00PM.

You do not need to bring plants to attend; but you do need to bring plants if you want to participate in the plant swap.

How It Works:

  • Space is limited, RSVP online.
  • Bring as many plants as you’re willing to trade (must be at least 1).
  • All plants must be pest-free.
  • Label the plants with your name and the plant name if known.
  • Place your plants on the table that matches your plants.
  • Plants can be potted, bare-root, or cuttings.

Swap Etiquette:

  • Talk to each other and agree on the swap; do not take a plant without permission.
  • No grabbiness. Be courteous. Have fun.
