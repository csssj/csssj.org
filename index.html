Welcome, Visitors!

The Cactus and Succulent Society of San Jose (CSSSJ) welcomes you to our website.

The CSSSJ is a diverse group of people with a mutual interest in and fascination for cacti and other succulent plants.

Succulent plants are among the most bizarre, beautiful, and varied in the entire plant kingdom. If you share our interest in these fascinating and unique plants or just want to learn more, we invite you to join us at our meetings and have some fun.

Upcoming Events

Monthly Club Meeting: Sunday, January 5th, 2020, 12:30 PM

Presentation: Staging

Joseph Hidalgo, incoming President of the Monterey Bay Area Cactus and Succulent Society and an active potter since 2011, will give a live demo on potting up plants for show purposes, also known as staging. Active members of the club know him as one of the potters who sells at shows and meetings: many plants have been staged using his high-fired stoneware pieces.

If you like, bring a plant that isn’t repotted, and that you intend to enter in a future show: he may choose it to use as an example for his repotting demo. For additional details, see the club’s Facebook page.
