Picture Submission


If you have won a ribbon for photography at one of the CSSSJ Shows, please submit your picture for inclusion on this page by email to webmaster@csssj.org.

Copy the following text into the Subject field: CSSSJ Submit Picture

Attach the picture to the email. Select the best file you have for the picture. (The file size will be adjusted to make it suitable for viewing on the web before it is uploaded to the web site.) If you do not have a computer readable file for your picture, contact me at webmaster@csssj.org, and we will make arrangements to scan it.

Include the following information in the body of the email:

Year of Show:
Season of Show (Spring or Fall):
Photographer:
Grower:
Comments:
(Include plant name if known)
