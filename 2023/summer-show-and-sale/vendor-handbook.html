Vendor Handbook 2023

Key Information About the Summer Show & Sale 2023:

Confirmation of Interest

  • Return a signed intention letter by the date that has been communicated to you by Peter, Sales Chair, to reserve table space. You can either email the signed intention letter to saleschair@csssj.org, or upload it to Contracts Summer Show and Sale 2023.
  • Upon receipt of your intention letter you will receive an acknowledgement by email.

Important Items To Note

  • The show will be “people’s choice”. You do not need to bring any show plants.
  • However, as a club member you can enter up to 10 show plants at your discretion, if you wish to compete in the show.
  • Vendors must sign up for work duties. The club will use SignUpGenius again: https://bit.ly/csssjVoljun23.
  • Up to four tables per vendor are assigned first come first served. Additional tables, if available, will be allocated depending on the order in which intention letters are received.
  • Roughly 58 eight foot and 15 six foot tables will be available for high-volume vendors given the planned layout.
  • Vendors must claim their allocated tables by 5 pm on Friday. Unclaimed tables will be released at that time by the sales chair to other vendors.
  • Vendors must be members of the club for at least three months before being eligible to participate in its show and sales events. You are eligible to be a vendor if you renewed your dues no later than last December. Please contact membership if there are any issues.
  • Checks will be cut to Vendors within 45 minutes of the sale closure on Sunday.

Items for Sale Specifications

  • The focus of the sale is on succulent and cacti plants.
  • Non-succulent house and garden plants should therefore not be offered for sale.
  • Vendors should not sell wild-collected plants, or any plant that is illegal to possess.
  • Allied Arts, Custom Made Pottery, and Accessories will be available for sale.

Show

  • There will be prizes for plants that received the most votes in their respective categories.
  • As stated above, you do not need to bring any show plants. However, as a club member you can enter up to 10 show plants, if you wish to compete in the show.
  • Show items must be entered by 8pm on Friday and must stay in the show area for the duration of the show.

Participation Fee

  • All vendors are expected to contribute 25% of total sales proceeds to CSSSJ.
  • Vendors who sell their own pottery are expected to contribute 15% of total sales. Vendors are only eligible for this reduced rate if 90% of available inventory for sale is handmade artisan pottery.

Work Duties

  • Vendors need to perform work duties (cash register operation, security, etc.) based on the total number of tables allocated.
  • Each table requires 2 hour of work per day up to four hours per day. Vendors must sign up for work duties using SignUpGenius https://bit.ly/csssjVoljun23.
  • Volunteers from among CSSSJ will also be on hand to assist. All club members participate strictly on a volunteer basis and are not compensated for their duties.
  • Vendors are not eligible for Show Bucks.
  • Non-vendor substitutes for work duties are not allowed.

Setup and Breakdown

  • Vendors are expected to assist in the setup (starting 4:01 pm Friday) and breakdown (starting 3:30 pm Sunday) of the show & sales area.
  • Note that repeated failure by a vendor to follow the show rules or to comply with requests of the sale leadership, will result in the vendor receiving reduced table allocations, or being dropped altogether from future show and sale events, at the discretion of the Board.

Services Provided

  • Prior to the show and sale, CSSSJ will provide promotional materials to major media outlets, all social media channels, and event registration websites to help encourage public participation.
  • During the show and sale, CSSSJ volunteers will provide security, will call, and cash register help.
  • CSSSJ will provide cashier and credit facilities accepting Discover, VISA, American Express, MasterCard, ApplePay, and GooglePay.
  • CSSSJ will pay the applicable sales tax.
  • Pizza on Friday evening and lunch items as well as drinks will be provided for vendors and volunteers on Saturday and Sunday.

Bar Code Labels

  • All items for sale must have a barcode. Barcodes can be ordered in a streamlined process online: https://bar-code-labels.square.site/.
  • If you have problems with online ordering, contact saleschair@csssj.org.
