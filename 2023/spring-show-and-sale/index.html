Cactus & Succulent Society of San Jose

Spring 2023 Show and Sale

POSTPONED

We were notified, days before the scheduled beginning of the Show & Sale, that PG&E would be performing repairs that would cut Peterson Middle School’s power for most of Saturday.

We were actually prepared to go ahead despite that, and scrambling to secure the portable-battery gear that would allow us to proceed, but it turns out that regulations flat-out prevent our using the unpowered building even if we wanted to.

Unfortunately, there simply isn’t enough time to devise an alternate plan in half a week. We therefore have no choice but to postpone the event until such time as we can find a new venue. We’re sorrier than we can say — we were excited to see you, and we’ll let you know as soon as we’ve secured a new time and venue.
