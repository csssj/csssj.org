CACTUS AND SUCCULENT SOCIETY OF SAN JOSE
June 4, 2017 Board of Directors Meeting Minutes


Attending: Linda Delgado, Kathi Cambiano, Sonia Dyer & Roger Lane

Meeting called to order @ 11:00

Meeting was a round table of information/observations regarding the Spring Show and Sale (S&S)


Kathi:

  • She thought it would be nice to provide help in the wait line for checking out.
    • Someone to answer questions
  • She felt that it looked unprofessional to have people just sitting in the food/plant hotel area by the back doors to the court-yard.
    • Linda explained that it is a resting place for people and that we answer questions all weekend long from that location. We also provide security.
    • Linda also reminded the board that we are a social club and not a "business."
  • She thought that it was a bad place for the hanging bars to be where they were.
  • She thinks that it would be nice to have a clear stage for the winner’s plants. Pictures and such would look better if they were in a nicer setting.
  • Three cash-register would be better.
  • It would be nice to have a templet for S&S entries available.
  • Social media is so important now, maybe something like cut-outs for picture taking would be nice.
  • Jackie may have some new ideas for the post cards.
  • "Ask-Me" buttons would be nice.
  • Maybe we could have a place holder on the Show side for the entries that have been selected as First place and moved to the stage?


Linda:

  • Really great turn out. Could this be because of the later date, great weather or publicity? Maybe all three?
  • It would be nice to get more volunteers from the club. Many of us volunteered the whole time.
  • Vendors were still setting-up when the S&S had opened. Peter addressed this and it should be resolved.
  • The Sale side seemed to be open way before 9:00. They were told that they would not be able to check out before 9:00 and that the vendors were still busy setting-up. All seemed okay with this, but we will continue to watch and make sure that it doesn’t become a problem.
