Russell Wagner - Mesembs

Little Sphaeroid Press founder, Russell Wagner, holds a PhD from the University of California, Berkeley, and, for seven years, was the editor of the Cactus and Succulent Journal. His publishing concept is both traditional and modern. The aim of Little Sphaeroid Press is to produce beautiful printed volumes on a wide range of succulent-plant topics complemented with an engaging and informative web site.

Mesembryanthemums are succulent natives of the deserts of Southern Africa.
