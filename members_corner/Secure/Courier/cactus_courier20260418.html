 57th Annual San Jose Spring Show & Sale

We invite you to the San Jose Cactus & Succulent Societys 57th Annual Spring Show & Sale! Our spring sale will be held at Peterson Middle School. Were planning a full cactus, succulent, and caudex show on the stage again this year, featuring nearly 100 entry categories.

Invite your friends, family, neighbors, and acquaintances to help us spread the word! Learn, explore, and get expert advice from experienced growers in our nonprofit club community.

Find rare, collector-grade plants you wont see at big box stores. Dont miss the South Bays biggest cactus & succulent event! Come early for the best selection, with a fresh restock of plants available Sunday morning.

Enjoy free admission to our cactus show and discover rare xerophytic plants from around the world. Fun for all ages, bring the whole family!


Location:
Peterson Middle School, 1380 Rosalia Ave, Sunnyvale, CA


Show and Sale Schedule:

    Set-up: 7:00am - 10:00am
    Enter Show Plants (see article below for more info): 7:00am - 11:00am
    Members Only Sale: 10:00am - 11:00am
    Public Sale: 11:00am - 7:00pm


Sunday, April 19th
    Public Sale: 9:00am - 2:00pm
    Show plant pick up: 1:30pm - 3:30pm
    Clean-up: 2:00pm  4:00pm
