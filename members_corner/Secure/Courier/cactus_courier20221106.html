Preparing for Winter

Temperatures are dropping and it’s time to start thinking about how to protect our succulents and cacti. Our November meeting will focus on the best ways to do this. A limited amount of frost cover material will be available.


(Note: Unfortunately, Stephen McCabe will be unable to do his presentation in November due to family commitments. We look forward to hearing his presentation at a future date.)


COVID precautions: Masks are encouraged, but optional for our meetings.
