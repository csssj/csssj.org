Show & Sale Prep Roundtable

Our April meeting will start with an unusual hands-on practice session with Kathi Cambiano on how to use your smartphone or tablet to access the club’s new Facebook and Instagram pages. Be sure to bring your devices, preferably with both apps already installed. If you need directions for how to do that, please see the directions in last month’s issue. If you already know this stuff, come prepared to assist your neighbors.

This will be followed by a Show & Sales roundtable led by Loyd Monaco. Have you ever thought of selling some of your plants on the “member-table” at the sale? Or perhaps you have a few spectacular specimen plants you would like to see win a prize in the Show? Come prepared to ask your questions, and share your experiences.



There will not be a Mini Show at this meeting.




The meeting will be held in the Meeting Hall
