Hands On Seed Starting


With our society meeting again in person we will get hands on this month with a part three of our "growing from seed" series. The presentation will be an in-person, interactive workshop, in which attendees will watch a seed sowing demonstration and then sow their own cactus and succulent seeds. Materials for the workshop will be provided by the club, including soil, pots, and a variety of interesting and unusual cactus and succulent seeds.

Plan on being hands on with soil. We welcome club members bringing any left over or collected cactus and succulent seeds to the meeting to share with fellow growers.

COVID precautions: For the safety of all in attendance, please plan on wearing a mask while indoors.
