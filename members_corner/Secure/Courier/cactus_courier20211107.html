Cissus Tuberosa

This month's presentation will be on Cissus Tuberosa. An interesting North America native succulent that is easy to grow, easy to propagate, and can form a fabulous caudex.

We will cover potting, general care, and how to form a caudex. So, please join us to look at this under appreciated succulent presentation by Loyd and Judi.
