 Mega Show and Tell

Hear ye! Hear ye! The Mega Show and Tell is here! Renee Gunn will lead this exciting meeting.

Bring your favorite, least favorite, the one with the best story, most challenging, or most successful cactus or succulent for the Mega Show.

This has been a popular event in the past and gives everyone a chance to learn something new, get advice on a particularly difficult plant, or simply show off their collection.

Everyone who brings in plants for the Mega Show and Tell will receive one raffle ticket per plant (up to a maximum of five tickets, though youre welcome to bring more plants) for a special raffle featuring surprise prizes.






The meeting will be held in the Multi Cultural Center
