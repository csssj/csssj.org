"Plantstagram 101 (or Using Social Media to Share, Connect, and Learn)" with Sara McPherson

As a follow-up to her "Plant Photography 101" presentation in September 2023, Sara McPherson will be discussing how to use our fabulous plant photography skills on social media in order to exchange tips and tricks, show off our prized collections, connect with others who love plants as we do (including experts in the field), and enrich every day with fun and informative micro learning experiences.

Bonus: Celeste Paradise will make a brief presentation "CSSSJ Vendors: Tips and tricks, plus key requirements for being a vendor in 2024"



COVID precautions: Masks are encouraged, but optional for our meetings.
