Field Trip to Plant and Pottery Outlet

Plant and Pottery Outlet is located at 6467 Mission Road, Sunol, CA 94586. (925-862-0151) They have a reasonable selection of non-cactus succulents and a wide selection of pottery.

From 680 North, take the Andrade Road Exit, then turn left at the stop sign. Just beyond the freeway, turn left onto Mission Road. Plant and Pottery Outlet is one block up, on the left hand side.

For those who choose not to go on the Club’s trip to Plant and Pottery Outlet, there will be an alternate gathering at Prusch at the usual meeting time and place. It will not be a typical meeting (no mini show or speaker), but there will be time to discuss opportunities for members to help out the club. We will also be doing some fun activity, prepping for our Spring show and sale.
