Field Trip to Marc Beckstrom's Garden

Field Trip to Marc Beckstrom's Garden, Livermore
1538 Lomitas Ave, Livermore, CA
Sunday, October 5th, 11:00 AM – 12:30 PM


Our October meeting will be a field trip to a wonderful private garden in Livermore. Marc Beckstrom has a backyard garden that rivals many botanical gardens. He has collected some of the rarest cactus specimens, with an emphasis on naturally occurring plants.

CSSSJ members only, please, for this field trip. Post in our Facebook group to organize carpooling.


Fall Show and Sale

CSSSJ will host our Fall Show and Sale this October. This event will feature the first full judged cactus and succulent show in many years. The show will take place on the Peterson Middle School stage, with the main floor dedicated to the plant sale.

We already have 34 vendors signed up, and we are excited for the opportunity to host this special event. Thank you for being a part of making it happen!
