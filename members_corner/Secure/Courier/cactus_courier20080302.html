Peder Samuelsen - Cacti of Patagonia

Peder Samuelsen will be making a presentation on the cacti of Patagonia, Argentina. Several of our society members accompanied Mr. Samuelsen on this tour. Although there are not as many cacti and succulents to see as you would see in northern Argentina, the scenery is magnificent and there were some cacti.
