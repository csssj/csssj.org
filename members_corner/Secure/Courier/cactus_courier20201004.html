Newsletter of the Cactus and Succulent Society of San Jose

Due to the Shelter-in-Place order, we cannot predict when regular meetings will resume.

No October Meeting


October is normally when we have our Fall Show & Sale. Sadly, that cannot happen this year. So, we will not have an October meeting in 2020. There are other virtual activities available this month. (See details later in this issue.)

Keep well and hold on. We will get back to normal club activities eventually. Take inspiration from our plants. Succulents are masters of patience. Many cacti can wait a year or more for water without ill effects. Some of them reach tremendous heights growing only an inch or less a year.
