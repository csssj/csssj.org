Succulent Watercolor Workshop

Wendy Soneson will do a watercolor demonstration for 45 minutes, then give people a chance to try the paint. At a table in the back she may take six or so people trying watercolor at a time for 45 minutes. Six to eight minute sessions will allow 30 people to try painting.

Wendy Soneson has been teaching watercolor for 45 years, and in the Bay Area for 3 years. She recently had a one woman show at Oakland City Hall. She was the John Singer Sargent Watercolor demonstrator for the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston, where she taught before moving west. Her degree is in the Arts in Human Development, MA from Lesley University. See her website at watercolorweekly.weebly.com for classes and other shows.
