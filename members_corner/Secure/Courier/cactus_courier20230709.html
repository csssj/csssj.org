Country Store

If you have been to our Country Store in the past, you know this is one of the most fun events of the season. It is a great opportunity for you to share your bounty with your fellow members. Bring items that you think other members would enjoy. If you have a loud voice, we would appreciate your help in calling out the numbers at each of the tables!

Special Note: Due to space limitations in the Meeting Hall, we will be asking that if you plan on attending please RSVP by emailing your name(s) and phone number to: programs@csssj.org

Here is how it works:

  1. Separate tables will be set up for each category (cacti, succulents, misc. items (books, pots, rocks, etc), and desserts. People place their items on the corresponding table. If you bring desserts, make certain they are individually wrapped.

  2. Tickets will be available for purchase, 5 for $1, you decide how many you would like to buy. These are two part tickets, you keep one and put the other in pot (more in a moment).
    Because the ticket numbers can be hard to hear, please print your first, last name, or initials on each ticket before placing in the pot.

  3. You get to choose where you want to deposit your tickets now. Whatever table that you’d like to win some items from, put tickets in the pot at that table.

  4. When the event begins, volunteers will be stationed at each table to pull a series of tickets out of the pots. Those with the winning tickets from that table get to select an item from that table. This process continues from table to table until there are no more items or time runs out.

We hope to see you there for this annual event.





The meeting will be held in the Meeting Hall
