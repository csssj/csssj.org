Volunteering for the Fall Show and Sale

Due to the short lead time for the Fall Show and Sale, we’ve decided to postpone Russell Wagner’s presentation until sometime in 2023.

The focus for the meeting will be to familiarize members with the various ways to volunteer for our Fall event. You will be able to ask questions, and sign up for shifts during the meeting. One of the best parts of the Show and Sale - besides the amazing plants - is meeting and connecting with people!

A great way to make new plant friends is volunteering to the many fun positions that we have to put on a spectacular event. We are a fun and quirky bunch and love meeting new people.

Come join us and also get some of the benefits that are only open to volunteers:

  • Early access to purchase plants on Friday before the sale is open to members and general public
  • As a show of appreciation, coupons called "Showbucks" are given based on hours worked and are used to purchase plants
  • We feed you!
  • Endless conversations on plants and life!
Sign up for as many slots as you'd like! Use the online sign up form at https://www.signupgenius.com/go/9040445A5AA23A5FA7-csssj or email (Bobbie) bborn129@gmail.com and (Annie) aflau913@gmail.com about your interest.



COVID precautions: For the safety of all in attendance, please plan on wearing a mask while indoors.
