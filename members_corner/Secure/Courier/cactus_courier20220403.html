CSSSJ Inaugural Plant Swap!

When: Sunday, 4/3, 12:30-2:30 PM
Meeting Hall will be open around 12pm. Space is limited. If capacity has been reached, you may be asked to wait to enter.

*COVID Precautions: for the safety of all in attendance, please wear a mask when indoors.

Agenda
12-12:30 Let’s get reacquainted!
12:30 General meeting begins
1:00 Plant Swap commences
2:30 Raffle and Cleanup

Note: Plants may be brought in until 1:00PM

Location: Prusch Farm Park, Meeting Hall, 647 S King Rd, San Jose

You do not need to bring plants to attend; but you do need to bring plants if you want to participate in the plant swap.


How It Works:

  • Space is limited, RSVP online at https://bit.ly/3DbopuF
  • Bring as many plants as you're willing to trade (must be at least 1).
  • All plants must be pest-free.
  • Label the plants with your name and the plant name if known.
  • Place your plants on the table that matches your plants.
  • Plants can be potted, bare-root, or cuttings.


SWAP ETIQUETTE

  • Talk to you each other and agree on the swap; do not take a plant without permission.
  • No grabbiness. Be courteous. Have fun.
