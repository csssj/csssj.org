Get to Know Your Club

Come to the January meeting and discover all that your Club has to offer. You may have seen the mini show* at our meeting, but have you ever entered? We’ll discuss our lending library of books and social media presence. Just in time for National Seed Swap day on Jan. 28, we will be making seeds available from the club seed bank*. There will be an opportunity to discover the various ways you can give back to the club as some members will be retiring from longtime service. We look forward to seeing you on January 8th in the Multi Cultural Center (AKA the barn).

*benefits for current members of CSSSJ



Note: Due to a scheduling issue with Prusch Park, the meeting will be held in the Multi Cultural Center rather than our usual Meeting Hall. Parking is limited, please plan on parking in the larger parking lot (see map.)


COVID precautions: Masks are encouraged, but optional for our meetings.
