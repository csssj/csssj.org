Kalanchoe – Mother of Millions


Love them or hate them, this month`s presentation is on the Kalanchoe. An interesting species of succulent, loved for being flowering plants and interesting foliage plants, or hated as a nuisance. We will cover the care and the propagation and general overview of these diverse species.

The presenters are our own Loyd Monaco and Judi Ganschow, long time succulent enthusiasts and active club members.
