Turn One Succulent Into Many: The Ultimate Guide to Propagating Like a Pro!


James Endo will share his propagating experiences and help you grow succulents like a billionaire!!! No leaf is unplanted in his mind, no leaf left behind, and he will give you some "How-Tos" so you can grow your collection to trade, share, and sell! This presentation will be great for beginners and experience. Also, if you have a succulent you want to propagate but are afraid to chop, then bring it and James will review your best options for success! Let James be your succulent coach for the day so you can propagate like a pro!!! James can also do some "Succulent 911" so bring a couple you want to save.


Lanelle of @claygurlcreationz will be bringing her very unique pots to sell at the meeting.

(Although we had hoped to offer a special cactus fruit tasting at this meeting, we will need to postpone it for a future meeting.)
