 Pelargoniums

What is a Pelargonium? Species Pelargoniums are primarily found in Africa with a handful of species making it to eastern Africa, two in Turkey and a few in Australia. Pelargoniums are commonly mistaken for geraniums, though relatively different in flower morphology. This Sunday, Joseph Hidalgo will be speaking on Pelargoniums with focus on the succulent species and geophytes.

He will be bringing pottery and pelargoniums to sell.
