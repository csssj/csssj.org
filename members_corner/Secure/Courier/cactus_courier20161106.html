Share What You Love!

Our November meeting features an expanded "show and tell" Mini Show and pot luck! Have you ever wondered what judges are looking for when they look at a plant? Do you know the best way to stage a plant? Do you have a favorite plant you'd love to boast about? Then this special Mini Show event is for you.

Each CSSSJ member, though, will still earn Mini Show points for the plants you bring. You can bring a maximum of 5 plants, and we'll still vote as usual for winners in each category. But as a bonus, the winner in each November Mini Show category will win a pot!

Be prepared to discuss the plants that you bring for these categories, and don't forget your questions:
      Cereus
      Sanseveria
      What did you do with your Raffle/Country Store plants?
      Sedum

We look forward to seeing what you bring in! And bring in your favorite pot luck dish to share.
