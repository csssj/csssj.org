Staging for the Show

Joseph Hidalgo will give a live demonstration of potting up plants for show purposes, also known as staging. If you have been active in the club, you may also know him as one of the potters that sells at shows and meetings. He has been an active Potter since 2011 and many plants have been staged using his high fired stoneware pieces.
