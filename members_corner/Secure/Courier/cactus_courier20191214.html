Holiday Extravaganza

This year's Holiday Banquet will be held on Saturday, December 14 from 11:00am to 3:00pm. It will be at Peterson Middle School, 1380 Rosalia Ave, Sunnyvale. Tickets are required. Members who have not already purchased a ticket will not be able to attend.

Events include:
      Lunch catered by Maggiano's Little Italy
      Gift Exchange
      Silent & Live auction
      Table prize

Members who renew at or before the December meeting will participate in a special plant raffle.
