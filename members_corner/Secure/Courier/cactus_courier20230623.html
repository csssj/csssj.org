Summer Show and Sale

Our Summer Show and Sale will be at a temporary replacement for our usual venue: Fammatre Elementary School, 2800 New Jersey Avenue, San Jose, CA 95124. We start setting up no earlier than 4 pm on Friday 6/23. (See first article for more details.)

Friday (June 23, 2023):
  4:00pm - 6:30pm: Set up for Vendors
  5:00pm - 7:00pm: Show Set-up
  6:30pm: All Show entries need to dropped off for staging
  6:30pm - 8:30pm: Volunteer Appreciation Sale

Saturday (June 24, 2023):
  7:00am - 8:00am: Set-up for Vendors
  8:00am - 10:00am: Member Shopping
  8:00am - 5:00pm: Show Voting
  9:00am - 10:00am: Other Societies Access
  12:00pm - 1:30pm: Potluck/Lunch
  10:00am - 5:00pm: Public Sale Time

Sunday (June 25, 2023):
  8:00am - 9:00am: Set-up for Vendors
  12:00pm - 1:30pm: Potluck/Lunch
  2:00pm: Show Winners Announced
  9:00am - 3:30pm: Public Sale Time
  3:30am - 5:00pm: Clean-up

There will be prizes awarded for the entries that are most popular with our visitors.

Show entries may be picked up starting at 3:30pm.

Remember to bring in all the boxes you can find that are suitable for plants. You get .05 per box in show bucks to spend!

Note that you must be a member volunteer to access early shopping on Friday and Saturday.
