Keith Kitoi Taylor - Staging Your Plant

Keith Kitoi Taylor is a ceramic pottery maker and longstanding succulent plant enthusiast.

Staged: Combining plant and vessel to create a trophy winning presentation.

Keith will bring in specimens from his collection and talk about what makes an eye appealing presentation. In addition, he will talk about growing mediums and conditions; so have your questions ready.

Pottery will be available for purchase.
