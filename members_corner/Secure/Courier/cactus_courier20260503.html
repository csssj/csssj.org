 Field Trip to Moran Nursery, Watsonville

Sunday, May 3, 2026 | 10:00 AM  12:00 PM

Location:
Moran Nursery
420 Browns Valley Rd
Watsonville, CA

For our May Cactus and Succulent Society of San Jose meeting, we will be taking a field trip to Moran Nursery, a wholesale cactus and succulent nursery in Watsonville. Moran is a large, wholesale-only operation that is normally closed to the public. The sprawling greenhouses are well organized and feature a wide variety of beautiful, well-grown plants. You will have the opportunity to tour this working nursery and shop for plants at wholesale prices.

A 10% discount is available for cash payments, which is recommended. Credit and debit cards are also accepted.

Please note that the greenhouses can get very hot, so it is recommended to bring water with you.



Free nursery cart rides from Loyd.
