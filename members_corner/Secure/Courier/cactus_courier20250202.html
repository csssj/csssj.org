Get to Know Our Cactus Club - Meet & Greet, Volunteer Opportunities, Club History, Plant Giveaways

We will be celebrating the new year for the CSSSJ.

Come get to know your fellow club members. Learn the history of the CSSSJ (64 years old!). Find out about opportunities for volunteering and participating in club plans for 2025. Join the discussion on planning the Show and Sales for the year. Enjoy some plant giveaways for club members.

Club members are also always welcome to join the CSSSJ Board Meeting as a member at large. Board meetings are held at 10:30am, before the regular club meetings.
