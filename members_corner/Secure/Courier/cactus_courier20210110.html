Show Us Your Garden


Since we are unable to tour each other’s gardens in person, we will do so virtually this month. For our January meeting we'll be doing a slideshow of members' plants. If you'd like to contribute photographs of your own collection or garden, please send them, along with any descriptions or notes, to archivist@csssj.org.

The meeting will be on Zoom. While the meeting proper will begin at 12:30, we’ll have the Zoom session started, so that folks can join in early and catch up with one another, shortly after noon.
